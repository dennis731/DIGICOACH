Meet Your Coach, Dennis Wickstead

Hello! I'm Dennis, and I'm passionate about helping people feel confident and capable with technology. I believe that everyone should have the skills to connect with loved ones and access the amazing resources available online.

I use my experience as a FIFA B licensed coach and my skills in Agile and Co-operative coaching to create a supportive and effective learning environment. We'll tackle your tech challenges as a team! My goal is to make learning tech feel less like a chore and more like a fun, empowering journey.

I am also proud to hold a Community Coach Developer Award, which reflects my commitment to helping our community grow.
